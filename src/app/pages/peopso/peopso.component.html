Progam Educational Outcomes(PEO)

    Be experts in the field of computer science and engineering with sound knowledge and hands-on experience in the latest technologies, excellent problem-solving skills, along with a strong desire to learn and update themselves and be able to devise the best solutions for all the engineering problems they encounter.
    Develop the ability to associate and work in teams, have profound communication skills, and exceptional leadership skills that benefit them to grow as leaders or entrepreneurs.
    Be committed to society, responsible to take care of the needy, and behold personal and professional ethics in all their endeavours.

    Use professional engineering practices, methods, and skills to develop efficient computing solutions.
